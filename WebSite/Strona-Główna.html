Break the Fake is an app that helps you read only true articles.

Our mobile app is built to check credibility and sentiment of the articles served to you. Seek truth in your newsfeed. Read no more fake news. Get the most of your time spent on reading online newspapers.

Filter out fake news

Use Break the Fake to choose the articles you read based on credibility score.

Our advanced algorithms work for you to filter out fake news. You decide how high the score should be.

Learn the article's sentiment 
before you read

Every article you check gets evaluated gets assigned in one of three categories:
* positive
* neutral
* negative

Archive valuable information

You can save the articles you've searched. Read or analyze them later. Send to your co-workers, partners, friends and family.

Share the most interesting news with your network on social media. Help others by your own sentiment evaluation.
